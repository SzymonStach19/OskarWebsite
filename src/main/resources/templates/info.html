Oskar is a Director and Photographer living and working in New York, but also available internationally.

He has expertise in beauty, fashion and still life, each crossing over seamlessly into moving image, stills and animation. His fantasy narratives and clean aesthetic shine throughout his body of work, creating trademark graphic and colour led visuals that are always optimistic and fun.

He can tailor his vision to whatever the production requires, being comfortable shooting on location or in the studio. His multi disciplinary skill set has established him as a versatile creative director.
